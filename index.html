About

I'm an Industrial Technology graduate majoring in Computer Technology from Talisay City College, Cebu, with a growing focus on graphic design and digital content. Alongside my studies, I spent about three years freelancing, designing logos, posters, and social media content for local shops and small businesses. During my internship at Renix Enterprises, I designed promotional graphics and product visuals for collectibles, posters, and figurines, while also creating social media content that helped drive engagement for an online store.

I bring together technical know-how — from computer hardware to networking fundamentals — with a creative eye for branding and visual storytelling, which lets me move comfortably between design work and the technology that supports it.
